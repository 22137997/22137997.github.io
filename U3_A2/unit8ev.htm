About the App:
The App I have created is a simple alphabet app, targeted towards to children in between the ages of 3 and 7. Its letter range is in between of A to F, perfect for young learners, as well as a kid friendly design, it helps capture the attention of children without distracting them form the main function of the app. It consists of eight pages, the first is the homepage, allowing the child to start the app with a pressing a simple green button. The next six are the letter pages, A to F, each containing a voice line, an image of an animal or object starting with them and being able to draw on them. The last page is the end page, where the child can either start again or quit the app entirely.

Overall, the app is a simple alphabet learning game, where it can help children with learning their letters.
