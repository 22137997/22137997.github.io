Introduction:
Unit 8 is the unit all about designing and creating a mobile app, primarly us making an app targeted towards young children. We first started off analysing two apps with very different functions and purposes. The first one was Whattsapp, where we identified the purpose of the app, user requirments, user friendliness, dependence on particular hardware, interface elements, integration with standard operating software, platforms and compatibiliy, as well its strength and weaknessess. We then did the exact same for the second app, which I have chosen: Now TV.


In the second assignment, we started planning and designing our app. We were tasked to create an alphabet learning app, from letters A to F, mainly targeted towards children, we were tasked to design the indiviual pages, each exapling what they do, there names and rejected ideas. We then designed outr own letters and sounds, I created my letters in paint 3D, as well as my own voice lines via an IA speaker website. To finish off, we had to fill in a tes plan, explaining what component will be assest when the app is completed, checking for any faults.


In the last assignment, we were tasked to create our app in MITApp Inventor. I followed my planned designs out near perfectly, including the sounds, images and components needed. We also had to show proof of our work as well as the block components that were used to structure the app within the website. After my app was compleetd, I obtained feedback from it and used it to develop my skills in the future.
