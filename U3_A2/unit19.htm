Introduction:
Unit 19 is all about computing in the workplace, demonstrating the qualites for what is need for an IT styled job. In assignment one, we were tasked to creat powerpoints for different interpretations of the characteristics of an employee, like descriptions and statistics on what your strengths are and what you can do to help the job.

For assignment two, we created a CV and a cover letter for a job example we were given, acting as if we were applying for the job in reality. mWe also had to fill out the multiple job forums as well, like name, date, strengths, educational background etc, as well as a combined document of the CV and cover letter for easier access.

In the last assignment, assignment three, named; How a workplace utilises communication technology, we were tasked to fill in multiple documents explaining what how certain companies utilies different communication technology, as well as filling out multiple question sheets about the interesting equipment.
